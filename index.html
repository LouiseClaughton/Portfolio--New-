About Me

I'm Louise Claughton, a web designer and developer, with a focus on creative and visually inspiring websites. I graduated from Leeds Arts University with a Bachelor of Arts, and decided to combine my love of the visual medium with my love for technology and problem solving. I took the Web Design and Development Bootcamp from the Coder's Guild to help build some hard skills, and enjoy learning new technologies and ways of doing things.

When I'm not coding websites, I enjoy playing video games and reading, especially fantasy and sci-fi which have a focus on storytelling and worldbuilding.
